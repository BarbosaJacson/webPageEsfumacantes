About Me

1. The Engineering Core: Safety & Precision

My professional foundation was built on rigorous technical standards and risk management. I began my career as an Occupational Safety Technician, a role that demanded extreme attention to detail. To deepen my expertise, I earned my degree in Environmental Engineering and specialized as an Occupational Safety Engineer, amassing over 18 years of experience in managing complex industrial and environmental systems.

2. The Financial Pivot: Algorithmic Logic

In 2019, after three years of deep study into financial markets, I transitioned into active trading. This journey led me to the intersection of finance and technology, where I developed automated trading robots, simulators, and execution systems using Nelogica's NTSL language. This experience in algorithmic thinking was the primary catalyst for my evolution into professional software development.

3. The Tech Evolution: Full-Stack Mastery

Driven to master the architecture behind the code, I pursued a Postgraduate degree in Systems Analysis and Development, concluding in December 2025. My technical toolkit includes Java (OOP), SQL, and MongoDB. Currently, I am specializing in Full-Stack Web Development through Udemy, integrating my engineering precision with modern frameworks and a dedicated CSS/JavaScript Roadmap.
